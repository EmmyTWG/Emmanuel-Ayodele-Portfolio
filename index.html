My Process

How I design things

A disciplined, repeatable process that balances speed with depth — because good design is both a craft and a method.

01
Discover

User interviews, stakeholder alignment, competitive analysis, and defining the real problem to solve.

02
Define

Synthesising insights into personas, jobs-to-be-done, and a sharp design brief.

03
Design

Low-fidelity wireframes → component library → high-fidelity screens in Figma with interactive prototypes.

04
Test

Usability testing, design critique, iterating on findings until the experience feels inevitable.

05
Deliver

Dev-ready handoff with annotated specs, a design system, and post-launch metrics tracking.
